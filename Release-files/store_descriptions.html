Full Description

For Google Play. Also useful as a base for App Store marketing copy.
Watt helps people understand, calculate, and shop for solar systems with more clarity. Instead of guessing what size system you need, Watt gives you practical tools that make solar planning easier step by step. You can estimate loads, calculate panel and battery needs, review inverter and wiring choices, and build a clearer picture of the system that fits your home, project, or budget. With Watt, you can: - Estimate your solar system needs with calculators built for real solar decisions - Use quick tools for panels, batteries, inverters, wires, and water pump setups - Build a more complete design with the System Calculator Wizard - Prepare organized offer requests before contacting providers - Browse solar products and compare options in the storefront - Explore services and companies that can help you move from planning to execution - Manage your profile, notifications, feedback, password reset, and account deletion inside the app Why Watt is useful for end users: - It turns technical solar questions into simpler guided steps - It helps you avoid buying components blindly - It gives you a better starting point before speaking to installers or sellers - It brings planning, request preparation, and product browsing into one place - It supports both English and Arabic for easier everyday use Watt is useful whether you are: - trying to understand how many panels you may need - comparing battery and inverter sizes - planning a pump or household setup - preparing to buy a full system or individual components - looking for a clearer, more organized way to start your solar journey If you want an easier way to design, calculate, and buy a solar system with more confidence, Watt gives you the tools to start well and choose more carefully.
